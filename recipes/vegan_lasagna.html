Vegan Lasagna

Description

A mouth watering vegan lasagna, easy, healthy and fast.

Ingredients

  • Olive Oil
  • 2 Onions
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic
  • Can of tomatoes
  • Lasagna sheets
  • 2 Courgettes
  • 200gr Cashew nuts
  • Black pepper
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Soak the cashew nuts for 2 hours.
  2. Chop the onions and saute until soft. Add the garlic and cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Put the courgettes in a food processor and grate. Add the courgette and the herbs and spices and cook for 3 minutes.
  4. Add the cashew nuts to the food processor with an equal amount of water. Mix until a fine paste. Add to the sauce pan and mix.
  5. Assemble the lasagna, start with a layer of tomato sauce, then the courgette mixture and then the lasagna sheets. Repeat until you reach the top of the oven dish
  6. Bake the lasagne in a pre-heated oven (200 degrees) for 40 minutes.
