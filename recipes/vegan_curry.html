Vegan Curry

Description

A delicious vegan curry with cauliflower and potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cauliflower
  • 300 gr potatoes
  • 1 bell pepper
  • 1 onion
  • can of tomatoes
  • can of coconut milk
  • curcuma
  • garam masala
  • a thumb of fresh ginger
  • 3 garlic cloves

Steps

  1. Slice the onion and bake until soft, add the minced garlic and minced ginger and the spices. Saute for five minutes.
  2. Add the diced potatoes, cauliflower, and bell pepper saute for 3 minutes. Then add the can of tomatoes, mix everything well and cook for another 5 minutes.
  3. Add the coconut milk and cover the sauce pan with a lid and cook for 20 minutes on a medium fire.
  4. Serve with your favorite rice.
