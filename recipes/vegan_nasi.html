Vegan Nasi

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 leek
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Bell pepper
  • 2 Carrots
  • 1 Bokchoy
  • 400 gr white rice
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • a thumb of ginger
  • a thumb of galangal
  • curcuma
  • coriander
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. cook the rice
  2. Chop all vegetables. Add the chopped onion to a large wok. Saute until soft, grind the garlic, ginger galangal and add to the wok together with the other spices.
  3. Add the other vegetables and saute until soft. Add the rice, bake until lightly colored and serve.
